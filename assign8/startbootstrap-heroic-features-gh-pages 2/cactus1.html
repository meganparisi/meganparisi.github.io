Astrophytum myriostigma

Also known as "Bishop's Hat," this columnular cacti starts off as a small, rounded plant, and grows taller as it matures. The plant is gray-green, with white tufts of “hair” instead of spikes along its ribs. It produces sweet smelling yellow flowers after it reaches maturity.
